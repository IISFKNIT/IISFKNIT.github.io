IISF KNIT, Sultanpur

It's enough to have a vision, We will take care of the rest!

Welcome to our IISF KNIT, a dynamic hub for innovation, creativity and entrepreneurship. Our center is a vibrant community where startups, entrepreneurs and innovators come together to collaborate, learn and grow. Our mission is to provide the resources, mentorship and guidance necessary to help turn great ideas into successful businesses.

Our Incubation Center offers a wide range of services designed to support startups and entrepreneurs at all stages of their journey. From coworking spaces to funding opportunities, mentorship to training programs, we provide everything needed to help entrepreneurs turn their ideas into thriving businesses. Our experienced mentors and industry experts are on hand to provide guidance, advice and support, while our state-of-the-art facilities offer the tools and resources needed to bring ideas to life.

Our Facilities

Events Organised by IISF, KNIT, Sultanpur.

  • 01 Coworking Spaces:

    We provide startups with affordable and flexible coworking spaces to help them save costs and work in a collaborative environment. Our coworking spaces are equipped with all the necessary amenities such as high-speed internet, printers, and meeting rooms.

  • 02 Mentorship and Networking:

    We offer startups access to a network of experienced mentors who provide guidance and support in various areas such as fundraising, marketing, and operations. Our incubation center also offers various networking opportunities for startups to connect with investors, customers, and other entrepreneurs.

  • 03 Training and Workshops:

    We organize regular training sessions and workshops to equip startups with the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed. These sessions cover a wide range of topics such as business planning, financial management, and product development.
