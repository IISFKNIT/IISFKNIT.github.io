Global Investor Summit

“The intelligent investor is a realist who sells to optimist and buys from pessimists” - Benjamin Graham



Uttar Pradesh Global Investors Summit(UPGIS) 2023 was organized in Lucknow from 10-12 February 2023. The summit witnessed an undoubtedly colossal audience comprising government leadership, investors, Students, think tanks, and businessmen from all over the globe. The main aim behind it was to explore future opportunities in the field of Startups and investments. UPGIS was also successful in sponsoring hundreds of Startups and local businesses to provide them entrepreneurial guidance. UPGIS 2023 initiative follows the vision of the Hon’ble Prime Minister of India to lead the Nation towards a USD 5 Trillion economy. To meet this vision, the UP government has set up a target of making the state a 1 Trillion dollar economy in the five years to come. Overall it was very well coordinated and concluded with a positive promise for the country's future.
